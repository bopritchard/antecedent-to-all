## How to Use These Prompts

1. Open Cursor Chat
2. Paste `prompts/session-boilerplate.md` first
3. Paste the relevant prompt from above
4. Specify that you're working on `research/doj-structural-safeguards.md`
5. Emphasize originalist/textualist approach
6. Ask Cursor to evaluate constitutional legitimacy, not just describe current practice

## Key Questions to Explore

- Are structural safeguards constitutionally legitimate?
- Do they conflict with unitary executive principle?
- Do they serve legitimate purposes or undermine democratic accountability?
- What would original understanding say about these safeguards?
- Can Congress constitutionally limit executive control?
- How can we balance accountability and insulation from misuse?

## Connection to Core Themes

### Original Authority
- What would framers have thought about structural safeguards?
- Are safeguards contemplated by Constitution or post-constitutional construction?

### Unitary Executive
- Do safeguards conflict with unitary executive principle?
- Does Constitution require or allow independent executive departments?

### Democratic Accountability
- Do safeguards enhance or undermine popular sovereignty?
- How do they affect electoral accountability?

### Constitutional Drift
- Do safeguards represent drift from original understanding?
- Have they maintained form while changing function?

### Separation of Powers
- Do safeguards respect or violate separation of powers?
- Can Congress constitutionally limit executive control?
